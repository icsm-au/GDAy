Geographic to projection (spheroid to grid) formula

The formula used by GDAy to convert geographic coordinates of latitude, longitude and ellipsoidal height (f, l, h) to Transverse Mercator projection coordinates of easting, northing and zone (E, N, zo) is Redfearn's formula (Redfearn, Empire Survey Review, No. 69, 1948). The formula is accurate to better than 1mm in any zone of the MGA and for all practical purposes may be regarded as exact.
