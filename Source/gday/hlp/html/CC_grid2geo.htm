Projection to geographic (grid to spheroid) formula

The formula used by GDAy to convert Transverse Mercator projection coordinates of easting, northing and zone (E, N, zo) to geographic coordinates of latitude, longitude and ellipsoidal height (f, l, h ) is Redfearn's formula (Redfearn, Empire Survey Review, No. 69, 1948). The formula is accurate to better than 1mm in any zone of the MGA and for all practical purposes may be regarded as exact.
